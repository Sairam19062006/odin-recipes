Rasgulla

Back to Homepage

Description

Rasgulla is a one of the famous sweet recipe in India popular for its sweerness and its sphere shaped appearance....!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Rose Water
  • Cardamom

Steps

  1. Boil the milk and add vinegar to it to curdle it.
  2. Strain the curdled milk using a muslin cloth to separate the whey.
  3. Knead the chenna (curdled milk) until smooth and make small balls.
  4. Boil sugar and water to make a syrup, then add the chenna balls.
  5. Cook until the balls expand and become spongy.
  6. Add rose water and cardamom for flavor.
  7. Let it cool and serve chilled.
